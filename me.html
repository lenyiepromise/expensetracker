About Expense Tracker

Expense Tracker is a simple and intuitive app designed to help you manage your finances effectively. Track your income and expenses, view detailed reports, and gain insights into your spending habits.

Features include:

  • Easy-to-use interface
  • Real-time balance updates
  • Detailed charts and reports
  • Secure and private data handling
